Who am I?

Well, allow me to introduce myself

My name is Dustin Sparks, I'm a 29 year old photographer born and raised in Atlanta. I specialize in landscape photography and portraits, although I am always looking to explore different styles and techniques. I have been intrested in photography from a young age, I decided to buy a cheap canon t3i and just start shooting, however I quickly outgrew this and progressed to the canon 7D, and finally the 6D that I use today. I am completely self taught in both shooting and editing. I enjoy traveling and capturing it all on camera.
