Who are we?

Here at Southampton White Taxi Excursions, we aim to give all our customers the best trip possible. Whether that be a quiet and comfortable journey to the airport, or an intresting and informative guide of Southampton and further afeild. We started this company two years ago, and have grown to become the leading taxi/tourst firm in Southampton. With many of our drivers having over 28 years of local knowledge in: Southampton, Salisbury, Stonehenge, New forest and many other locations.

We offer many different configurations to suit the type of journey you require. We offer vehicles with seating for up to eight people, meaning we can accomidate large parties of people. If you would like a qote from us, dont hesitate to get in touch via the 'contact us' page.

With many new and modern vehicles, you can rest assured your journey will be a comfortable one.

Even our largest eight seater vehicles accomidate step free access.
